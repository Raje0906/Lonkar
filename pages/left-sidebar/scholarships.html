Scholarships

Government Scholarships

  • Post Matric Scholarship

    For SC/ST/OBC students who have passed SSC examination

  • Merit-cum-Means Scholarship

    Based on academic performance and family income

  • National Means-cum-Merit Scholarship

    For economically weaker section students

Institutional Scholarships

  • Merit Scholarship

    For students with exceptional academic performance

  • Sports Excellence Scholarship

    For students with outstanding achievements in sports

How to Apply

  1. Fill the application form available at the school office
  2. Submit required documents
  3. Attend the interview (if required)
  4. Await results
